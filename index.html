dogs

The dog is a domesticated descendant of the wolf. Also called the domestic dog, it was selectively bred from an extinct population of wolves during the Late Pleistocene by hunter-gatherers. The dog was the first species to be domesticated by humans, over 14,000 years ago and before the development of agriculture. Lifespan: 10 13 years Gestation period: 58 68 days Daily sleep: 12 14 hours (Adult) Class: Mammalia Domain: Eukaryota
